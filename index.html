AI / Quantum Investment Ledger

An all-time, source-linked tracker of national AI/quantum investment announcements — one row per announcement — with per-capita / %GDP / %GBARD views and an FX-vs-PPP currency split. Each figure is tagged commitment-vs-outlay, public-vs-mobilized, source tier, status, horizon, and confidence before any comparison. Three views: List (every announcement, newest-first), By country (collapsible groups), and Compare (a normalized cross-country dashboard on a public-outlay spine). Click any country name to open its full profile card. The ledger is the product; the index is a later layer.

→ Provisional composite index (Stage 4)
How to read this — headline figures are NOT additive. The only defensible sum is appropriated public outlays (KPI below). Expand for the full guide ·
Most large headlines (Stargate, InvestAI, France) are private/mobilized capital or multi-year targets, not government outlays. PPP-blended applies market FX to the tradable share (compute/hardware) and PPP to the rest (talent/ops) — a sensitivity scenario, not truth. Normalization denominators (GDP/pop accurate; price-levels & GBARD approximate) are flagged for later pinning. Realization view compares what has actually been realized against the committed headline and a linear-schedule expectation, flagging pace (ahead / on track / behind / stalled) — obligated awards are not disbursed cash, so the basis is shown; most pledges are not yet tracked (statistics lag 1–2yr). The composite index is a deliberately later, PROVISIONAL layer. Partial seed (target: ≥40 jurisdictions).

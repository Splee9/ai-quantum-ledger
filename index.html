AI/Quantum Investment Ledger

A source-linked, downloadable ledger of national AI/quantum investment commitments, with per-capita / %GDP / %GBARD views and an FX-vs-PPP currency split. Each figure is tagged commitment-vs-outlay, public-vs-mobilized, horizon, and confidence — before any comparison. The ledger is the product; the index is a later layer.

How to read this — headline figures are NOT additive. Most large headlines (Stargate, InvestAI, France) are private/mobilized capital or multi-year targets, not government outlays. The only defensible sum is appropriated public outlays (below). PPP-blended applies market FX to the tradable share (compute/hardware) and PPP to the rest (talent/ops) — a sensitivity scenario, not truth. Normalization denominators (GDP/pop accurate; price-levels & GBARD approximate) are flagged for later pinning. Partial seed (target: ≥40 jurisdictions).
